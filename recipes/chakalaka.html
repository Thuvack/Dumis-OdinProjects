Chakalaka relish recipe

Description

Chakalaka is a favourite South African vegetable relish that is served with just about anything from meat, bread, pap, mashed potato and with stews. How hot should you make it? The hotter the better!

Chakalaka is delicious as a flavouring for stews and sishebos and you can make it in advance and store it in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 green peppers
  • 5 chillies
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of hot curry powder
  • 4 carrots
  • 6 tomatoes
  • 1 tin of baked beans in tomato sauce
  • 1 table spoon salt
  • 1 table spoon pepper

Steps

  1. Heat Â½ a cup of oil in a pot over a medium heat
  2. Dice onions, green peppers, chillies, cloves of garlic and hot curry powder and stir until the onions are soft
  3. Add grated carrots, diced tomatoes and a tin of baked beans in tomato sauce
  4. Mix well and allow to simmer on a medium heat for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
