Amanqina dish Recipe

Description

In rural South Africa people have been enjoying amanqina enkomo or trotter stew for generations. Some South Africans take this traditional gem of a meal for granted and have no idea how nutritious it is.

An amanqina recipe is high in protein, and contains gelatin, calcium, amino acids alongside important nutrients and minerals like magnesium, phosphorus and selenium.

Ingredients

  • 1kg cow heels
  • 1 cubes of beef stock
  • 1 table spoon of salt
  • 3lt water

Steps

  1. Put ingredients in large pot and slow cook for 2 hour to 2.5hours
  2. Check regularily to ensure that cow heels don't stick to the bottom of the pot
