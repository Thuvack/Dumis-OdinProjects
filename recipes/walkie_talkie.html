Chicken walkie-talkies recipe

Description

Chicken walkie talkies dish, is a simple yet scrumptuous South African dish made of chicken feet!

Enjoy the dish with pap, bread, or on its own, so long as you make sure it is served hot.

Ingredients

  • 45ml (3 tbsp) sunflower oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 5ml (1 tsp) crushed ginger & garlic
  • 30ml (2 tbsp) Robertsons chicken Spice
  • 300 grams chicken feet
  • 3 medium-sized tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 375ml (1 Â½) cups hot water

Steps

  1. Heat sunflower oil in a saucepan over medium heat, add onion and fry until golden brown in colour, 7 minutes.
  2. Add ginger & garlic, 1 tablespoon of Robertsons Chicken Spice, chicken feet and fry for 5 minutes, then add tomatoes and continue frying until your tomatoes become soft, 10minutes.
  3. Add the remaining Robertsons Chicken Spice, hot water, bring to the boil and simmer on medium /low heat until the sauce slightly thickens, 20 minutes.
  4. Add mixed veggies and cook for 7 minutes.
